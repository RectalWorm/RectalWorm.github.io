How to create a good prompt using my TIs

You're obviously free to experiment, but bear in mind that my TIs are trained with a more or less fixed phrasing, that normally starts with:

"photo of EMBEDDING_NAME, a woman"

So I recommend always starting your prompt like that and then building the rest of the prompt from there. For instance, "photo of (m3ln4via:0.99), a woman, RAW, close portrait photo, black leather jacket, tank top with logo, pale skin, slim body, (high detailed skin:1.2), 8k uhd, dslr, soft lighting, high quality, film grain, Fujifilm XT3 sharp focus, f 5.6, short pixie black hair".
