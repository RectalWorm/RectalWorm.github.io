This is the ultimate Hypernetwork for Luxury Modern Interior Design trained on the style of GDM with 9k steps, 260 images. This hypernet designed to be flexible and mixed into prompts instead of having a dedicated checkpoint.

RECOMMENDED SETTINGS SD 1.5:

Steps: 100

CFG: 11

Prompt Trigger Words: "<hypernet:gdminthn9k:1> a luxury modern interior design, xxxx room "

Replace the "xxxx" with living room, dining, bath, bed etc.

Tip#1: Try to use the above Steps and CFG settings first and tweak from there. I think 80 steps may be the minimum acceptable quality. CFG 10 is minimum and after 14, will be a hit or a miss. But you can try other settings to fit your needs.

Tip#2: Using prompt "a luxury modern interior design" works best to trigger the look, then add your own prompt to spice up the picture!
